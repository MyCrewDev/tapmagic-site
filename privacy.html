Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 March 2026

Tap Magic ("we", "us", or "our") builds mobile applications designed to run entirely on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our apps and our website at tapmagic.tech.

Our Core Principle

We believe your data belongs to you. Our apps are designed to process everything on-device. We do not operate user accounts, cloud services, or analytics servers. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location or GPS data
  • Photos, videos, or camera data beyond what is processed locally on your device
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Device Permissions

Some of our apps request access to device hardware features such as:

  • Camera — for augmented reality experiences and real-time visual processing
  • Motion sensors — accelerometer and gyroscope for interactive features
  • ARKit / Face ID sensors — for depth sensing and AR capabilities

All data from these sensors is processed locally on your device in real time. It is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If a future app includes any third-party service, we will update this policy and clearly disclose it in the relevant App Store listing.

Apple App Store

Our apps are distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain data as described in Apple\'s Privacy Policy. We do not have access to any data Apple collects through the App Store.

Children\'s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since our apps do not collect personal data from any user, they are safe for use by people of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since our apps do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no user data for us to delete. Any data generated by our apps exists solely on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy?

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our support page.
