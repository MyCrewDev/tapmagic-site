Frequently Asked Questions

Do your apps collect my data?

No. All of our apps process data entirely on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Our apps don't require accounts, logins, or registration of any kind. Just download and use them.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

Which devices are supported?

Each app's system requirements are listed on its App Store page. Generally, our apps support iPhones running iOS 17 or later.

How do I delete my data?

Since our apps don't collect or store data on any server, there is no account data to delete. Any local data created by our apps is removed when you uninstall the app from your device.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We appreciate bug reports. Please use the contact form above with details about what happened, including steps to reproduce the issue if possible.
